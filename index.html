Campus Keepsakes

(A Video Project)

"Campus Keepsakes" is a video project by Samira Aldybergenova, a student at NYU Abu Dhabi, that captures the essence of campus life through a short film showcasing various sceneries at different times of the day, accompanied by familiar background sounds.

From the hum of conversations and the clatter of dishes at D2, to the rustling of palm leaves in the wind, the video encapsulates the subtle, everyday moments that define the NYUAD experience.
